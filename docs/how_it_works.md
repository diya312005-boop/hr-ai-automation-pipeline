# How It Works

## Tools Used
- Claude AI — candidate matching and outreach
- LinkedIn — candidate sourcing and connection notes
- Google Forms — candidate screening form
- Google Sheets — stores all responses
- Google Apps Script — auto scoring and email sending
- Google Calendar — interview booking
- Gmail — automated candidate emails and nightly digest

## Flow
1. HR pastes a Job Description
2. Claude finds matching candidates on LinkedIn
3. Connection note sent with Google Form link
4. Candidate fills the form
5. Apps Script scores responses automatically
6. Candidate gets shortlist, review or reject email instantly
7. HR gets a nightly digest every evening
8. Shortlisted candidates get calendar interview slots booked automatically
